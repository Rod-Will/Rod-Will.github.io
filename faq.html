Frequently Asked Questions

   

      Find answers to common questions about my expertise, projects, and how I approach AI/ML challenges.    

   
           
       

          What specific AIML algorithms do you specialize in?           +        

       
          I have extensive experience with classification algorithms such as Logistic Regression, Support Vector Machines (SVMs), Random Forests, Gradient Boosting (XGBoost, LightGBM), and basic Neural Networks. For predictive modeling, I frequently use Linear Regression, Ridge/Lasso, and Time Series models. My focus is on selecting the most appropriate algorithm for the problem and data.        
     
           
       

          Can you work with industry-specific data formats (e.g., healthcare, engineering sensors)?           +        

       
          Yes, I have experience handling diverse data types common in engineering (time-series sensor data, logs, operational parameters) and healthcare (EHR data, claims data, vital signs). I am proficient in data cleaning, transformation, and feature engineering tailored to these domains.        
     
           
       

          How do you approach model deployment?           +        

       
          I follow MLOps principles to ensure models are production-ready. This typically involves containerization (Docker), setting up APIs (Flask/FastAPI), utilizing cloud services (AWS SageMaker, GCP AI Platform, Azure ML) for hosting and monitoring, and integrating with existing software systems.        
     
           
       

          What is your process for a new project?           +          

       
          My process usually involves understanding the problem, data exploration and cleaning, feature engineering, model selection and training, rigorous evaluation and validation, and finally, deployment and monitoring. Communication and collaboration with stakeholders are key throughout this process.
